The GEM-stage tower is a portable tower that is capable to provide the power needed for a full live show of a DJ. It can be fit in a container which can be carried by a semi-truck to the location and assembled there. 
- Study the foundation implementation (precast concrete blocks)
- Check connections and stability principles
- Assembly & transportation method
- Ask about the weathering effects on the steel members/ connections
